Plumbing Emergency Tips

  • Burst pipe: Shut off main water valve immediately
  • Clogged drain: Avoid chemical cleaners that can damage pipes
  • Water heater leak: Turn off water supply and power/gas to unit
  • Sewer backup: Avoid using any water fixtures in your home
  • Gas leak: Evacuate immediately and call emergency services

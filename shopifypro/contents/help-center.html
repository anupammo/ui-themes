ShopifyPro Help Center: We're Here to Assist You

Need help with your **order, returns, refunds, or shopping experience**? Our **customer support team is available 24/7** to ensure a hassle-free experience at ShopifyPro.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Track My Order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a **tracking link via email**. You can also visit the Order Tracking Page to get real-time updates.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

We support **credit/debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cash on Delivery (COD)** in select locations.

How Can I Cancel My Order?

If your order has not been shipped yet, you can **cancel it through your ShopifyPro account**. Visit **My Orders** and click **Cancel** before dispatch.

What Happens If My Order Is Delayed?

Occasionally, orders may face slight **shipping delays due to high demand or logistics**. If your order is **delayed beyond the expected delivery window**, contact our **support team**.

Returns & Refunds

How Do I Request a Return?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a return within **30 days of delivery**. Simply visit the **Returns Page** and follow the steps.

How Long Does a Refund Take?

Once we receive your returned item, **refunds are processed within 5-7 business days** to the original payment method.

Which Items Are Not Eligible for Returns?

Certain items like **personal hygiene products, digital downloads, and final-sale clearance items** may not qualify for returns.

Electrical Safety Tips

  • Never overload outlets or power strips
  • Use GFCI outlets in wet areas
  • Replace frayed or damaged cords immediately
  • Keep electrical devices away from water
  • Install tamper-resistant outlets if you have children
  • Have your electrical system inspected every 5 years

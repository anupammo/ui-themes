Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common travel planning questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

For domestic trips, we recommend booking at least 1-2 months in advance. For international travel, especially during peak seasons, 3-6 months ahead is ideal to secure the best options and prices.

Can I customize a package to suit my preferences?

Absolutely! Our travel specialists excel at creating tailor-made itineraries. Just let us know your interests, budget, and travel style, and we'll craft a personalized journey just for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by package. Most trips offer free cancellation up to 45 days before departure. We'll provide complete details before you confirm your booking.

Do you offer group travel discounts?

Yes! Groups of 6+ travelers receive special discounts. Contact our group travel coordinator for custom quotes and special group amenities.

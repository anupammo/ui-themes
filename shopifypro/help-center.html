Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about orders, payments, and more

How Can I Track My Order?

Once your order is placed, you will receive a tracking link via email. You can also visit the Order Tracking Page to get real-time updates on your shipment's progress.

Our tracking system updates every 6-12 hours, providing you with the latest information on your package's location and estimated delivery date.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

We support a variety of payment options for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal for secure online payments
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay for mobile payments
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) in select locations

All transactions are encrypted and secured for your protection.

How Can I Cancel My Order?

If your order has not been shipped yet, you can cancel it through your ShopifyPro account. Simply follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your ShopifyPro account
  2. Go to "My Orders" section
  3. Find the order you wish to cancel
  4. Click the "Cancel" button before dispatch

If the cancel button is unavailable, it means your order has already been processed and shipped. In this case, you can request a return once you receive the item.

What Happens If My Order Is Delayed?

Occasionally, orders may face slight shipping delays due to high demand or logistics challenges. If your order is delayed beyond the expected delivery window, we recommend:

  • Checking the tracking information for updates
  • Contacting our support team for assistance
  • Allowing an extra 1-2 business days for delivery

If your order is significantly delayed (more than 5 business days past the estimated delivery date), please contact our support team for further assistance.

How Do I Change My Shipping Address?

You can change your shipping address if your order hasn't been shipped yet:

  1. Log in to your ShopifyPro account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Select the order you want to modify
  4. Click "Change Shipping Address"
  5. Enter your new address and confirm

If the option is unavailable, it means your order has already been processed for shipping. In this case, contact our support team immediately for assistance.

How Do I Apply a Promo Code?

To apply a promo code during checkout:

  1. Add items to your cart
  2. Proceed to checkout
  3. Find the "Promo Code" field below the order summary
  4. Enter your code and click "Apply"
  5. The discount will be reflected in your order total

If you're having trouble applying a code, ensure it hasn't expired and that your order meets the minimum requirements for the promotion.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Our straightforward return policy makes it easy to exchange or return items

How Do I Request a Return?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a return within 30 days of delivery. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to your ShopifyPro account
  2. Navigate to the "Returns" page
  3. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  4. Choose a return reason
  5. Print the return label
  6. Package your item securely
  7. Ship it back to us

Once we receive your return, we'll process it within 1-2 business days.

How Long Does a Refund Take?

Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method. The timing for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment provider:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-10 business days after we process the refund
  • PayPal: Usually within 24 hours
  • Bank Transfers: 5-10 business days

You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.

Which Items Are Not Eligible for Returns?

For health and safety reasons, certain items are not eligible for returns:

  • Personal hygiene products (toothbrushes, earrings, etc.)
  • Digital downloads (software, ebooks, music)
  • Final-sale clearance items (marked as non-returnable)
  • Perishable goods (food, flowers, etc.)
  • Custom-made or personalized items

Please check product descriptions before purchasing, as some items may have specific return policies.

What Condition Should Returned Items Be In?

To be eligible for a return, your item must be:

  • Unused and in the original condition
  • In the original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase
  • Free from any damage, stains, or odors

We reserve the right to refuse returns that don't meet these conditions or to issue a partial refund if items show signs of use.

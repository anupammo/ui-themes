Frequently Asked Questions

You can request prescription refills through your patient portal, by calling our office during business hours, or by having your pharmacy send us a refill request. Please allow 48 hours for processing.

We accept most major insurance plans including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department at (555) 789-6543 to verify if we accept your specific plan.

You can access your medical records through our secure patient portal. If you need a physical copy, please complete our Medical Records Release form and submit it to our office. There may be a processing fee for physical copies.

For your first appointment, please bring:

  • Photo ID
  • Insurance card(s)
  • Completed new patient forms
  • List of current medications
  • Any relevant medical records
  • Payment method for copay or deductible

Yes, we offer telehealth appointments for many types of visits. You can schedule a telehealth appointment through our patient portal or by calling our office. Please note that some conditions require in-person evaluation.

About AutoVibe – Drive Your Passion

AutoVibe was founded with one goal: to create a car buying and ownership experience that’s built around people, not pressure.

We believe the joy of driving starts long before your engine turns over — it begins with trust, transparency, and passion.

Our Origin Story

Founded in 2015 by automotive enthusiasts with decades of industry experience, AutoVibe emerged from a simple idea: car buying should be an exciting journey, not a stressful transaction.

We started in a small garage with just five pre-owned vehicles. Today, we operate a 20,000 sq ft facility with over 200 new and pre-owned vehicles, but our commitment to personal service remains unchanged.

Our Mission

To deliver the best automotive experiences by offering high-quality vehicles, reliable service, and a community-driven approach that empowers our customers to drive with confidence.

Who We Are

We’re a tight-knit team of car enthusiasts, certified service professionals, and knowledgeable consultants who know how to match vehicles with drivers.

Whether you're buying your first car, upgrading for family needs, or maintaining your current ride — AutoVibe is here to make it simple, smart, and satisfying.

Our Journey

2015: The Beginning

Founded in a small garage with just 5 pre-owned vehicles and a vision for a better car buying experience.

2017: First Showroom

Opened our first dedicated showroom with 20 vehicles and expanded our service department.

2019: New Partnerships

Formed partnerships with 3 major manufacturers to offer new vehicles alongside our pre-owned selection.

2021: Community Awards

Received "Best Local Dealership" award three years running from City Business Journal.

2023: Expansion

Expanded to our current 20,000 sq ft facility with state-of-the-art service center.

2025: The Future

Launching our electric vehicle initiative with 15 new models and dedicated charging stations.
